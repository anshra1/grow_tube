Privacy Policy

LevelUp Tube

LevelUp Tube is a distraction-free learning app for saving YouTube videos, tracking watch progress, and resuming where you left off. This page explains what data the app uses and how that data is handled.

Effective date: March 27, 2026

1. Information We Use

LevelUp Tube is designed to use as little data as possible. The app may process the following information:

  • YouTube links you provide to add videos to your library.
  • Clipboard text when the app checks whether you recently copied a YouTube link.
  • Video metadata such as title, thumbnail, channel name, and duration, fetched from the YouTube Data API.
  • Playback progress so the app can resume videos where you left off.
  • Theme preference and basic local settings stored on your device.

2. What We Store on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your saved video library.
  • Your watch progress and last played position.
  • Your app theme preference.

This local data is used only to make the app function for you.

3. Clipboard Access

The app may read clipboard text to detect copied YouTube links and offer a quick “Add” or “Watch Now” prompt.

Clipboard text is processed locally on your device. It is not stored remotely and is not sent to our servers.

4. Network Requests and Third-Party Services

LevelUp Tube connects to third-party services only as needed to provide app functionality:

  • YouTube Data API v3 to fetch video details for links you add.
  • YouTube embedded player services to play videos inside the app.
  • Network connectivity checks to detect when your device is offline or back online.

These services may receive technical request data such as your device IP address as part of normal internet communication.

LevelUp Tube does not provide its own account system, does not require login, and does not include ads, analytics, or crash reporting in the current release.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data is only shared with third-party services when needed to deliver the app’s core features, such as fetching video metadata or playing embedded YouTube content.

6. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you remove it by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

LevelUp Tube is not specifically directed to children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact the app publisher using the support details listed on the app store page.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will be made available on this page with an updated effective date.
